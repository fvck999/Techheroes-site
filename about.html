We are sfindr. a team of young developers who saw a problem in organizing sport events

When the four cool dudes first started out, their passion for Sports and Esports drove them to start their own business. We hope you enjoy our service as much as we enjoy offering it to you. If you have any questions or comments, please don’t hesitate to contact us.
